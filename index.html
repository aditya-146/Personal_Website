    About Me

    I’m Aditya Sharma, a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo passionate about the intersection of hardware and software in embedded systems, robotics, and automotive technology. I’ve developed firmware for low-power RFID systems at the University of Alberta’s IWT Lab, contributed to UWaterloo’s Aerial Robotics team by building firmware for advanced power monitoring, and supported the Formula Electric team with Hardware-in-the-Loop testing systems. Alongside these experiences, I’ve built projects like a smart 4x4 RC rover, a portable STM32 diagnostics monitor, and an automated plant watering system, all of which reflect my drive to create reliable, efficient, and impactful technology.
